    Marketing Pitch:

  • Our project is designed mainly to predict the compressive strength behaviour of concrete. Concrete being a base material for any construction, it is designed to provide a high range of mechanical and durability properties and to meet the design requirements of a concrete structure. The compressive strength of concrete is the most common property and the measure used by the engineer in designed buildings and other structures if the concrete is structurally acceptable or not. It is often necessary to test concrete structures after the concrete has hardened and to determine if the structure is suitable for its designed use.

    We, as an Engineers team, always works for Safer Buildings, which we always dreamt of. A key to protecting yourself during disasters is finding safe shelter, whether it be at home, work or school. To maximize your safety, we are here to construct the building to properly withstand a disaster, especially if the building is in a high-risk location, such as an area prone to hurricanes or near earthquake fault lines. It’s an important issue, as many disaster-related injuries and deaths can be prevented when buildings are engineered by us to weather a catastrophe.

    There are been many testing methods to find out the compressive strength of Concrete, but we keeping a keen eye on determining its best compressive strength using the Machine learning models to predict the compressive strength of field concrete which is evaluated and compared to those models trained exclusively on field concrete data rather to laboratory concrete models. This analysis helps for building predictive models which is a promising method for reducing common over-prediction issues caused by laboratory concrete models, that are used in isolation. We as a team working hard to gain recognition on a large scale with this new technique as it provides contracting authorities with accurate and objective information for monitoring quality which is the control of concrete construction.
